Case Study

Ärzte Tracker

Stress-optimized medical appointment tracker built for daily use on mobile.

01

Problem

Managing medical appointments is unexpectedly stressful — especially for people with multiple doctors, recurring checkups, or family members to coordinate for.

Existing apps are bloated, require accounts, or are designed for clinics rather than patients. The result: people default to paper notes or scattered calendar entries, and miss appointments or forget to call.

02

Approach

Strip everything down to the essential task: know who your doctor is, when your next appointment is, and be able to call them immediately.

No sign-up. No sync. No notifications that need permissions. Just a fast, local tool that works offline, lives in the browser, and gets out of the way.

03

Design Principles

  • Reduce friction at every step
  • Surface the most urgent information first
  • One tap to call — always visible
  • No clutter, no settings screens
  • Works on the smallest mobile screen

04

Features

  • Today / Tomorrow smart labels on appointments
  • Auto-sort by nearest upcoming date
  • Click-to-call doctor directly from the card
  • Full offline support via LocalStorage
  • Mobile-first layout — thumb-friendly

05

Tech Stack

HTML CSS Vanilla JS LocalStorage

No frameworks. No dependencies. No build step. The entire app ships as a single HTML file.

06

Result

A focused, zero-friction tool that does exactly one job well. Loads instantly, works offline, and fits in a single file.

Built for real use — not for a portfolio. Designed around the actual stress points of managing medical care, not around feature checklists.
